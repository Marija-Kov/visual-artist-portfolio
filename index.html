about

You are hereby presented with another drop in the ocean of artists. That is fine because oceans are the strongest forces of nature and symbolize many things of value to humans.
A hopeless DIY-er wondering how many artists cringe writing about themselves in the third person, I'm probably out there coding something, being plotted against by a rescue pigeon or doing embroidery while watching telenovela 'cause I'm not wasting my time sitting and watching tv.
Some time ago I got a Masters degree in Arts that gives me an advantage in life sometimes. I switch the mediums I work with a lot and often am the first in the crowd to try and do something differently. I do my thing in Belgrade and its outskirts accompanied by a dog or few and supported by a loving partner on a daily basis.
