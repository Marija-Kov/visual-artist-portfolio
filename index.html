about

You are hereby presented with another drop in the ocean of artists, for a lack of better expression. Which is fine because you probably can't name one bad thing about the ocean off the top of your head. A hopeless DIY-er wondering how many artists cringe writing about themselves in the third person, I'm probably out there coding something, being plotted against by a rescue pigeon or doing embroidery while watching telenovela 'cause I'm not wasting my time sitting and watching tv. Some time ago I got a Masters degree in Arts that helps me in life sometimes and holds me back the other times. I switch the mediums I work with a lot and often am the first in the crowd to try and do something differently. I do my thing in Belgrade and its outskirts accompanied by a dog or few and supported by a loving partner on a daily basis.
